About Us

Service Line 4 You sp. z o.o. started its activity at the end of 2016 from trading Christmas trees in Germany. Specially selected trees from our trusted business partners from Denmark came to the delight of our customers to their homes during the beautiful Christmas season. Our Christmas trees came to homes of many people from different cultures who live in the Hamburg city. Christmas is special time for everyone and it is very important cultural tradition which expanded almost all across the world.

Our mission since the beginning is to provide services to customers with the best quality. We ensure our clients about the best quality of selected products which we offer for the market. We wish to be treated the same way we treat others - with
the greatest respect, trust, loyalty and honesty.

The quality of our services is demonstrated by the full commitment of each person involved in the work on behalf of Service Line 4 You sp.z o.o. Join us, work with us, buy from us in the name of a better future for us all.
